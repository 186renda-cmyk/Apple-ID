Fix "Account Disabled in App Store" 账号被停用？原因分析与解封指南

Error Message: "Your account has been disabled in the App Store and iTunes."

常见报错：“您的账户已在 App Store 和 iTunes 中被禁用。”

Why did this happen? 为什么会这样？

  • Risk Control: Your IP address changed frequently (hopping between countries).
    风控：您的 IP 地址频繁跳动（经常切换节点）。
  • Shared Account: You are using a cheap "Shared ID" used by 100+ people.
    共享号：您使用的是廉价的“共享ID”，几百人同时用，必封。

Solution: Contact Support 解决方法：联系客服

You can try to contact Apple Support via chat. Here is a template:
